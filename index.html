About US

IEDC VKCET is one of the most vibrant and active IEDC cell in Kerala. We conduct various activities in and out of the campus focused on student entrepreneurship.We facilitate the environment with the latest industry standard software and hardware. IEDC VKCET is supported and funded by Kerala StartUp Mission (KSUM).
Here are some of our initiatives...

Orientation Sessions

We organize orientation sessions in diferent colleges to make them aware about iedc and its activities. Orientation covers the major goals of iedc along with some interesting and mind-blowingsessions.

Technical Sessions

We arrange technical talks and hands-on workshops for schools and colleges, with an objective to impart some changes in the educational system. Contact us for an appointment.

Evening Caffe

When your good study habits go down the drain and your textbooks start to look as though they're written in runes, don't panic. Join us on Evening Caffe to get some hands-on learning experience.
